A Database Management System for Genealogy Societies!

Upload your genealogy records, media, and books and make them searchable on your society website.

Get started

Features

A better way for people to search your genealogy records

Give your users a state-of-the-art search interface.

Searchable records

Upload your records in spreadsheet (CSV) format. Specify which columns are names, dates, places, or keywords. You can even specify columns for relatives on vital or census records.

Images too

In addition to records, you can also upload images so images can be shown alongside the corresponding records.

Public or private - you decide

You can make your records public, limit search to members only, or make your records publicly searchable but limit record details or media to members only. It's your choice. Non-society members wanting to view members-only content get directed to your society registration page.

Integrations

A plugin is available to add a record-search form to any WordPress-based society website. Click here to see it in action. Integration with other websites is also possible; contact dallan@ourroots.org for details.
